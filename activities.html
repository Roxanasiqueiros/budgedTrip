San Francisco

Explore.



Lands End

A short hike from the parking lot walkdown a small trail. From the maze you can see the golden gate bridge and baker beach.

Cost: Free



Pier 39

Stroll down Pier 39 Visit local shops, grab some mini donuts at Trish's Mini Donuts and visit the local seals along the pier.

Cost: $$



Golden Gate Bridge Cruise

Take an hour cruise around beautiful San Francisco Bay and experience the Golden Gate Bridge from below.

Cost: $$



Lombard Street

Walk down the most crooked streeet in the world. Parking is limited, so be prepared to go on a little walk before getting to Lombard Street.

Cost: Free



Lake Tahoe

Pope Beach

Stop and take a breather. Soak in some sun and dip your toes in the lake.

Stand Up Paddle Boarding

Test your balance on a stand up paddle board. Glide through the clear water and soak in the scenery.

Hansen's Tube and Saucer

Looking for adventure? Try snow shoeing around Lake Tahoe. There's plenty of beginner to intermediate trails you can take charge.
